    \vspace{10pt}
    \refstepcounter{tablecounter}

\block{This is a title}{
The building blocks of the poster are ``blocks'' within columns. There are also subcolumns, inner blocks, and notes. Have a look at the documentation: \url{https://www.ctan.org/pkg/tikzposter}

Within blocks, you can do most of what you can do in any \LaTeX{} document. Special rules apply for figures and tables. See the examples in this document.

Unfortunately, the layout options are limited to rectangles within rectangles. If you wanted a spiral or tree layout, you're out of luck. 

As you might have guessed from the package name, the whole document is just a special kind of \texttt{tikz} figure. If you're familiar with \texttt{tikz}, you may have some luck manipulating the package code and creating more layout options or shapes. 
}
